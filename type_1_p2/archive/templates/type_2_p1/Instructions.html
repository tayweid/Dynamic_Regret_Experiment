Each Round you and the other participants in your group will participate in a grouptask. You and the other participants are given the choice to either contribute high H or low L effort. Whether you receive the high or the low reward depends only on the actions of the others in your group. Additionally, whether you incur an effort costdepends only on whether you choose H or L. The payoffs of the other participants inyour group are determined in the same way: whether they receive the high or the low reward depends only on the actions of the others in their group, and their effort cost depends only on their choice. If all other participants in your group contribute H, you receive a high reward {{ Constants.high_reward }}. If one or more participants in your group choose L, you receive a low reward {{ Constants.low_reward }}. If you choose H, you incur an effort costs of {{ Constants.effort_cost }}. If you choose L, you incur no effort cost.

Example 1. If you choose high effort, H, and all other participants choose high effort, H, you receive the high reward, {{ Constants.high_reward }}, and incur an effort cost, {{ Constants.effort_cost }}. Therefore, your payoff and the other participants payoffs this Round would be {{ Constants.high_reward }} - {{ Constants.effort_cost }} = {{ Constants.cooperate_payoff }}.

Example 2. If you choose high effort, H, and at least one other participants chooseslow effort,L, you receive the low reward, {{ Constants.low_reward }}, and incur an effort cost, {{ Constants.effort_cost }}. Therefore, your payoff this Round would be {{ Constants.low_reward }} - {{ Constants.effort_cost }} = {{ Constants.betrayed_payoff }}.

Example 3. If you choose low effort, L, and all other participants choose high effort, H, you receive the high reward, {{ Constants.high_reward }}, and do not incur an effort cost.
